About Me

Full Stack Web Developer, passionate about code.

Design

First I take your idea, applying the principles of the 5 planes of UX. I flesh out the project. What are you business goals? Who will use the site? Then carry out case studies of how people will percieve your idea.

Create

After the design phase, I take all the information gained and produce a wireframe/prototype of how your idea will look. We then discuss any changes and the best technology to use for your project.

Code

This is the stage where I turn your idea into reality. As a "Full Stack Developer" I make sure your web app looks beautiful, while ensuring what happens behind the scenes is fast, scalable and adheres to industry standards.
